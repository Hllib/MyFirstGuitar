Chapter 1
"As it began"
It all started in the winter of 2018 after the release of “Bohemian Rhapsody”. I must say that up to that time I was not a music lover and was not particularly interested in the world of music. Little did I suspect how much this movie would change my life...
I was largely inspired to buy the guitar by the lead guitarist of the band, Brian Harold May. His delivery of “Love of my life” in Tokyo, 2014, deeply shocked me. Personally, I also learned this guitar part and played it countless times.
Even though I listened to Queen before the movie, after watching it I became a real fan. I've listened to all of the band's albums, many of the songs are on my list of favorite pieces of music, and the band itself has become one of my most revered.
Chapter 2
"First steps"
By June 2019, I had saved up my money and headed to the store. By then I had studied a lot of information in the context of buying my first guitar and therefore I want to share my experience and give some advice
BUYING A GUITAR IS A VERY RESPONSIBLE AND IMPORTANT STEP, AND THERE ARE SOME KEY MOMENTS YOU NEED TO CONSIDER
Perhaps the first and most important step is to make sure that you really need a guitar. Of course, you won't know for sure until you try it, but it's definitely worth delving into the subject, weighing the pros and cons before going to the store. If you decide to buy a guitar - you need to decide on the price policy. It makes no sense to take an expensive first guitar, but you should not stop at the cheapest samples. The Internet is full of information about manufacturers. Choose a guitar according to the criteria prepared in advance, check its integrity, the distance between the strings, the uprightness of the neck, and of course the sound. Take care of your guitar, treat it like a live one, don't be afraid of it. You'll tear more than one string, you'll make mistakes, it'll be hard, but all this is nothing compared to the joy the instrument will give you over the years. And of course, practice. Every day. As much as you can.

Chapter 4
"Synopsis"
I am infinitely happy that in 9th grade I got the idea to buy a guitar. The guitar became not just my hobby, I really fell in love with it, it became something irreplaceable in my life. Guitar, like other musical instruments, develops brain, imagination, motor hand, sense of rhythm, musical ear, perception of the world and many other things. It gives endless amounts of pleasure and joy. This is the journey that never ends and you never want it to.
